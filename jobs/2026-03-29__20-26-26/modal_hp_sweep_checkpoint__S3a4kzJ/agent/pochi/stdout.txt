### Architecture

| Component | Details |
|---|---|
| `HPEvaluator` (Modal Class) | Stateless evaluator mounted with the checkpoint Volume; each instance scores one `(lr, batch_size)` pair |
| `.starmap(HP_GRID)` | Fans out **8 trials** (4 LRs × 2 batch sizes) in parallel across separate containers |
| `_write_checkpoint` | Remote function that serialises best params to the Volume and calls `.commit()` |
| `run_sweep` | Local entrypoint – drives the sweep, finds best trial, writes checkpoint, and populates the Dict |

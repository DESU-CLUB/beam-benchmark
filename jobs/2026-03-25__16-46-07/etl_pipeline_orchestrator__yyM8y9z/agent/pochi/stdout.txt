The ingestion component generates batches of raw records (each with a category drawn from a small fixed set, a numeric value, and a timestamp), persists them as JSON files on a **Modal NetworkFileSystem (NFS)** named `etl-nfs`, and immediately kicks off data processing for the new batch.
